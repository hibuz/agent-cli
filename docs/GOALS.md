# Agent‑Commerce Platform – Common Goals

1. **Rapid onboarding** – New agents can be registered and deployed in < 30 minutes.
2. **Reliable execution** – Workflow success rate ≥ 99.9% (with retries and compensation).
3. **Real‑time personalization** – Context‑aware recommendations delivered in < 2 seconds.
4. **Observability** – Every agent call automatically emits traces, metrics, and logs.
5. **Security & Compliance** – Data minimization, encryption at rest & in transit, PII pseudonymization, adherence to GDPR/CCPA/PIPA.
